About

Kumari Aunty is a popular street food vendor in Hyderabad, India who runs a stall near the ITC Kohinoor junction in Madhapur.Her popularity soared after a YouTube video by an influencer went viral. Operating her food stall at the ITC Kohinoor junction for over 13 years, Kumari Aunty's fame reached far beyond Hyderabad, thanks to the reach of social media.As hundreds of YouTubers waited eagerly for her return on Saturday afternoon near the thatched-roof eatery, her first reaction was a heartfelt request to allow her to do her business and earn a living. “Enough is enough,” she said. Four days ago, on January 30, the local traffic police asked her to shut the shop and move to another suitable location as swelling crowds outside her stall often caused major traffic blocks in the area. The street near ITC Kohenur is thronged by young IT professionals, drivers and daily workers alike every afternoon.
